Infinity Stone Collector

Can you collect the Infinity Stones before Thanos does?


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are 4 Infinity Stones below. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number displayed. If your total score exceeds the random number total you lose the game and Thanos wipes out humanity.


The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each stone, goodluck!
